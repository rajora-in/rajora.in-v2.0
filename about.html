About Me

My Story & Philosophy

My journey into embedded systems began with a childhood fascination for how things work. That curiosity led me to pursue a degree in Electrical Engineering and eventually to build complex, low-level systems. My professional philosophy is simple: **build robust, maintainable, and efficient solutions.** I believe that a great product is a result of meticulous design, rigorous testing, and a deep understanding of the hardware-software interface.
